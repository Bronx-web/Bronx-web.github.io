How much does a tradie website cost in NZ? +
A professional 5-page tradie website from Bronx Web starts from $2,900 NZD + GST. This includes mobile-first design, online booking, Google Business optimisation and basic SEO setup.

The Start Up package includes 5 custom pages, mobile-first design, online booking, basic SEO and Google Maps. The Premium package adds advanced quoting systems, stronger branding, review collection and 1 month free support.
Do I pay GST on top of the price? +
Yes. All Bronx Web prices are quoted exclusive of GST, which is standard practice for NZ businesses.
How long does the build take? +
Typically 2 to 4 weeks from your first strategy call to a live site. The biggest factor is how quickly you can provide content, photos and feedback.
What do I need to provide? +
Your logo if you have one, basic details about your trade and services, and photos of your work. If you don't have photos we can work around it. Bronx Web handles the rest.
Can you write the content for my website? +
Yes. Bronx Web can write all the copy for your website as part of the build. You just need to tell us about your business and the services you offer.
Are you based in Hamilton or offshore? +
100% Hamilton-based, Waikato, New Zealand. Not offshore, not a template company. Every site is built locally and customised for your trade business.
What trades do you build websites for? +
Lawn mowing, property maintenance, brick and block laying, plumbers, electricians and vehicle service stations across New Zealand.
Do I need a website if I have Facebook? +
Yes. Facebook does not rank on Google. When a customer searches for a tradie in Hamilton, Facebook pages rarely appear in results. A website gives you a permanent, searchable presence that Facebook cannot provide.
What's the difference between a GBP and a website? +
Your Google Business Profile shows you on Google Maps. Your website ranks in search results and builds credibility. Both work together — your GBP drives map traffic, your website converts visitors into enquiries.
How does a website help me get more jobs? +
A well-built tradie website works 24/7. It ranks on Google, captures enquiries while you're on the tools, builds trust through reviews and past work, and lets customers book without needing to call.

Yes. Bronx Web is based in Hamilton, Waikato and specialises in websites for NZ trade businesses. We serve Hamilton, Waikato and wider New Zealand.
